Codingal is an online platform designed to teach kids coding through engaging and interactive courses.
 It offers a wide range of coding programs tailored to different age groups and skill levels, focusing on fundamental programming concepts and creative problem-solving.
  With a curriculum aligned with standards like K-12 and built by experts,
   Codingal ensures that children not only learn to code but also develop critical thinking, 
   computational skills, and creativity. Through live classes with qualified instructors, 
   coding challenges, and projects, the platform fosters a fun and enriching e
   nvironment for young learners to explore the world of technology.
